    • ABOUT US

    Tech Aura is a National level annual technical fest organised by School Of Engineering And Technology, Nagaland University to promote technical consciousness in the mind of young generations. Derived from the words "Technology" and "Aura", it aims to spread an atmosphere of technological presence to the society. Every person has an AURA in them; an AURA of potential; of ideas and world changing solutions.It is in this potential and ideas we aim to tap and present to the society through the various technical competitions, presentations, exhibitions, and seminars. From rock bands, cultural shows, dance and music. Tech Aura is a celebration of life, culture and technology. Dont miss out! Come be a part of this celebration.
